Boys II Men

Boys II Men is an American R&B vocal group known for their smooth harmonies, emotional ballads, and major influence on 1990s music.

Official Website: https://www.boyziimen.com

Group Members

  • Nathan Morris
  • Wanya Morris
  • Shawn Stockman

Favorite Albums

Cooleyhighharmony (1991)
This debut album introduced Boys II Men to the world with hits like "Motownphilly" and "End of the Road." It combines New Jack Swing with classic R&B vocals and helped redefine vocal groups in the 1990s.
II (1994)
This album focuses more on romantic ballads and vocal harmonies. Songs like "I'll Make Love to You" and "On Bended Knee" made it one of their most successful releases.
Evolution (1997)
Evolution shows the group maturing musically, blending traditional R&B with a more modern sound. Tracks like "4 Seasons of Loneliness" highlight their emotional depth and vocal strength.
